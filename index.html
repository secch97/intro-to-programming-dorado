About Me:

I'm a person really passionate about computers since an early age and an in-training Web Developer. I graduated from Universidad Don Bosco in El Salvador, Central America, with a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Engineering on 2021. I have experience in IT and knowledge in the development of desktop applications and database modeling and design.

Currently, since I immigrated to the United States of America, I am in the process of switching my career towards web development because it is an area that makes use of creativity, teamwork and problem solving, things that I am very passionate about. For this reason, I am currently enrolled in Code The Dream, a free intensive training in software development to people from diverse low-income backgrounds. Code The Dream offers a pathway to careers in technology for people who might not otherwise have the opportunity to pursue tech jobs.

My ultimate goal is to become a Front End Web Developer that is capable of making intuitive, attractive, responsive and, above all, accessible web designs; and at the same time help, with my knowledge, people who are starting their path in the world of programming.
